About the Lodge

A nature retreat
with city convenience

Named after the birds that call the vlei home

Princess Vlei Lodge sits on the edge of one of Cape Town's most beloved urban wetlands — a haven for pelicans, blue cranes, sunbirds and starlings. Each unit is named for a resident species and styled for relaxed, nature-connected living.

The property features a shared swimming pool, a tiered timber sun deck, and landscaped lawns that blur gently into the reeds. Whether you're here for a weekend getaway or a longer stay, the lodge offers honest hospitality without unnecessary fuss.

  • Swimming pool shared by all units
  • Tiered timber sun deck with loungers
  • Fully equipped kitchens in every unit
  • Private entrances & bathrooms throughout
  • Secure off-street parking
  • Princess Vlei Nature Reserve walking trail access
  • Braai / BBQ facilities on site
